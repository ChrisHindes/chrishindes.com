Systems Engineer | Data Governance Engineering | Azure, Data Migration, Workflow Automation, and Enterprise Data Systems
About
I’m a Systems Engineer focused on data governance, enterprise data systems, and process automation. My work centers on building reliable, scalable solutions that improve how data is moved, managed, archived, and governed across internal platforms.
I’ve worked across cloud and on-prem environments with a strong focus on Azure, data migration, metadata integration, workflow automation, and operational reporting. I enjoy solving problems that sit at the intersection of systems engineering and data governance, especially when the work requires connecting technical details to broader process and business needs.
A big part of my role is turning complex requirements into practical solutions. That includes improving archiving and retention processes, supporting data integration efforts, designing frameworks for metadata and disposition handling, and helping teams create more consistent, supportable ways of working.
I bring a hands-on, collaborative approach to engineering. I’m comfortable digging into technical issues, creating structure where there is ambiguity, and driving work forward through documentation, process improvement, and cross-team coordination. I’m especially motivated by work that strengthens data reliability, reduces manual effort, and helps teams operate more effectively at scale.
